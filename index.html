Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

How fast is the deployment process?

Our infrastructure is optimized for CI/CD, allowing for near-instantaneous deployments after a Git push. We use global CDN for maximum speed.

Can I use a custom domain?

Yes, absolutely. Custom domain configuration is fully supported, including free SSL/TLS certificates via Let's Encrypt for all your projects.

What about security?

Security is our priority. Our infrastructure is protected by advanced firewalls, and all communications are encrypted. Repositories are isolated and constantly monitored.
